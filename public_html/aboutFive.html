ABOUT FIVE

Who are we?

Mission Statement

FIVE (Five Innovators Valuing Excellence) is a collaboration between growing thought leaders who are becoming subject matter experts in each of the five critical pillars of business. We discuss broad topics that exist within all companies - from startups to our own real world experiences. We wield distinct, intuitive input from a wide range of young, innovative professionals

  1. Strategy - Vision, culture and product development
  2. Finance - Capital and debt management
  3. Growth - Marketing, Sales and Business Development
  4. Legal - Intellectual Property, Corporate Structure
  5. Technology - Development, Process Management and Analytics

We are constantly evolving our theories through experience and reconciliation with new innovations so that we can blend the tried and true approaches with the newest and most innovative discoveries for potent, actionable concepts. We aim to convey these concepts outward in a manner that allows them to be used by any businessman. We want to contribute and lead in the development of innovative ideas and successful business concepts.

The idea of well rounded businessmen running a well rounded business is at the core of what we do. We bring all aspects of a business to the table and then we discuss the interactions between those disciplines in a way that lends itself to growth and prosperity. One person with little knowledge about a lot of things won't live up to their fullest potential, but Five people, each with a vast amount of knowledge about one area allows for a solid, well rounded foundation of knowledge upon which to build ideas and solutions. We have tried these out in our own careers at key positions within companies and starting our own.

We are builders who are focused on creating bright futures with increased possibilities for all. We endeavor to create self sustaining, timeless ideas that are or can be profitable enterprises. We are committed to delivering sound solutions to relevant business problems.

We are diverse. We are skilled. We are FIVE.
